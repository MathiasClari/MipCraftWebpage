FAQ

Who are our partners?

Our partners are groups of fair people who are giving free resources for their communities!

How to become a partner?

In order to become a partner you will have to message our owner PxLib via email partner@mipcraft.eu. After you send a message you will receive a message with short form. After you complete the form you will be qued to become our partner

Why you should become our partner?

We offer ingame/website promotion for our partners, Partners will also receive a special Partner rank

Do I need to pay to become a partner

NO! Our partner program is 100% free

NOTE: Partners are not our first priority! It usualy take us up to 7 weeks to accept the partnership

SkessySMP

SkessySMP, also written as SkessySMP, is a whitelisted Minecraft community. It was founded by PxLib (Skessy) in 2019, and has continued growing with many vanilla and modded seasons, the most recent one being Season 2022. The server is currently administered by PxLib.
